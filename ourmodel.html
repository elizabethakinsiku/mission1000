How We Work

Partnership Model

Individuals, churches, NGOs, organizations, and schools can partner with Mission1000 by getting the book for their teens for a token.

Sponsorship Model

Sponsors can give any amount or choose to sponsor teens by state, country, church, or school. Every seed goes directly toward printing and distribution.
