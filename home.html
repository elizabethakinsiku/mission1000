Our Story

Short, Honest, Grounded

Mission1000 didn’t start as a book project. It started as a burden; for teens, for foundations, for identity, for souls.

After years of working with teens across communities, schools, and rural areas, it became clear that transformation must begin early. Mission1000 exists as a God-inspired strategy to introduce teens to the Father’s love and build strong foundations for life.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of teens who know God personally, live with clarity of identity, and grow into wise, skilled contributors to society.

Our Mission

To introduce teenagers to God as a loving Father through the distribution of The Father You Never Knew book, nurturing a personal relationship with Him.

To support teens spiritually, educationally, and through skill development initiatives that prepare them for purposeful living.

To build a global ecosystem that drives long-term teen transformation through discipleship, mentorship, and empowerment.
