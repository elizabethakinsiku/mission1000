Frequently asked questions

Good to know before you partner.

The books are distributed to teenagers across Nigeria through secondary schools, churches, youth conferences, and community outreach initiatives. Our goal is to ensure that teenagers, regardless of their background, have the opportunity to discover their identity and purpose through The Father You Never Knew.

We work with trusted institutions and organizations that already have meaningful access to teenagers, including churches, schools, youth ministries, NGOs, and outreach organizations. Mission1000 also has dedicated State Ambassadors who coordinate and oversee book distribution within their respective states to ensure the books reach the intended beneficiaries with transparency and accountability.

Absolutely. You can choose to sponsor books for your state or any state that is close to your heart. Whether you're sponsoring a few copies or helping us reach the goal of 1,000 books in a state, your support directly contributes to putting life-changing resources into the hands of teenagers.

Yes! We welcome partnerships with businesses, churches, NGOs, foundations, schools, and other organizations that share our passion for raising a generation with a strong sense of identity and purpose. As a valued partner, your organization will be recognized on our website, acknowledged across our social media platforms, and featured in project updates as we celebrate the collective impact we're making together.

There are many ways to get involved beyond financial support. You can serve as a State Ambassador, help coordinate distributions, connect us with schools, churches, youth organizations, and potential sponsors, or use your skills and network to help amplify the vision. No matter your role, you'll be joining a community of people committed to helping teenagers discover who they are before the world defines them. If you'd like to volunteer, we'd love to hear from you.
