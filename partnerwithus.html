Partner With Us

Help Us Put Life-Changing Books in the Hands of Teenagers

Across schools, churches, and communities, thousands of teenagers are searching for identity, purpose, and a real relationship with God.

Through Mission1000, we are reaching teenagers with the message of God's fatherhood using the book The Father You Never Knew, a guide that helps teens discover God as a loving Father and learn how to build a personal relationship with Him.

But we cannot do this alone.

Partnership makes it possible for this message to reach more teens in more places.

Why Partnership Matters

Every time a teenager encounters God during an outreach, conference,
or school program, a seed is planted.
But growth requires guidance and continuity.
That is where The Father You Never Knew comes in..
This book helps teenagers:
  • Understand the Fatherhood of God
  • Build a personal relationship with God
  • Grow spiritually through guided confessions and reflections
  • Find answers to questions about faith and identity
  • Step-by-step guide for studying the Bible

Through partnership, we are able to produce more copies and distribute them freely to teenagers during school outreaches and teen programs.

Our Partnership Model

Mission1000 operates a simple and sustainable partnership model. Individuals, churches, organizations, and ministries can get copies of The Father You Never Knew for ₦1000 per copy.
This contribution helps us:
  • Continue the production of the book
  • Fund distribution during school outreaches
  • Reach teenagers who may never be able to afford the book

While partners receive the book for their teen conferences, youth programs, outreaches, and communities, Mission1000 continues to place copies freely in the hands of teenagers in schools.This means your partnership creates a ripple effect of impact.
